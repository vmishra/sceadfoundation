Our Mission

To promote environmental sustainability, education, health, and community development among marginalized communities across India — empowering underprivileged children, tribal villages, women, migrant workers, and elderly citizens.

Our Vision

To build a sustainable, inclusive India where every child has access to education, every community has clean sanitation and green spaces, and every woman and elder lives with dignity and financial independence.

Who We Are

SCEAD — Space for Children in Education, Art and Development — is a registered NGO founded in 1998, working across Karnataka and Tamil Nadu to uplift underserved communities through grassroots action.

Our Focus Areas

We work across three core pillars to create lasting, sustainable change in India's most underserved communities.

Environmental Sustainability

  • Large-scale plantation drives across Bengaluru, Anekal, Jigani, Bannerghatta, and rural areas
  • Lake rejuvenation initiatives restoring water bodies
  • Carbon sequestration verified by CERA (Carbon Emissions Reduction Assessment), Mumbai
  • Climate change mitigation through green cover expansion

Education & Child Welfare

  • Education support for government school children from disadvantaged backgrounds
  • School enrollment drives and educational material distribution
  • Anti-child labour awareness campaigns
  • Blood group identification and basic health screening for students
  • HIV orphanage (Home for HIV) and Girl Child orphanage programs

Community Development

  • Sanitation and toilet construction for tribal communities and slum dwellers
  • Livestock distribution to tribal families in Karnataka
  • Skill development and tailoring training for women
  • Elderly care services in tribal, rural, and urban communities
  • Disaster & crisis management support
  • Community welfare, peace initiatives, and social harmony programs

Our Story

Founded in 1998 by Siju Thomas Daniel, SCEAD Foundation India — Space for Children in Education, Art and Development — is a Bengaluru-based non-profit dedicated to environmental sustainability, social justice, community empowerment, and disaster & crisis management. Over two decades, we have successfully reached over 500,000 beneficiaries across India.

From planting 200,000+ trees, rejuvenating lakes, and building 500+ sanitation units in tribal villages, to adopting 35 government schools, training 300,000+ women in livelihood skills, and supporting 15,000+ tribal beneficiaries through livestock distribution — SCEAD's work is verified by CERA Mumbai for carbon sequestration and backed by partnerships with Genpact, Ascend Telecom, and Anthem Biosciences.

Our work has earned recognition from the Governor of Karnataka, the US Secretary of State, former UN Environment Executive Director Mr. Erik Solheim, and diplomatic missions from Italy, Japan, Germany, Canada, UK, Australia, and France. SCEAD Foundation is 80G & 12A approved, CSR-1 verified, with 100% clean audit histories.
