Meet the Artist

Mariamma James is a retired nurse with over 35 years of healthcare experience who pursued her lifelong passion for painting after retirement. Under the guidance of multiple art teachers, she has become accomplished in both acrylic and oil painting.

Her work specializes in landscapes and the natural world — forests, rivers, sunsets, and village scenes rendered in rich, vibrant oil colours on A3 canvas boards. Each painting captures the beauty of India's diverse geography.

By purchasing her art through SCEAD Foundation, you're not just decorating your space — you're directly supporting HIV-affected children at Snehalaya Home, girl child education, migrant worker welfare, differently abled programs, and children's blind schools.
