Art for a Cause

Buy original oil paintings. 70% of every sale supports SCEAD Foundation's work with HIV-affected children, girl child education, and community programs.

Support Through Art

SCEAD Foundation's online art gallery is a unique fundraising initiative where beautiful original paintings are sold to directly support our communities. 70% of every sale goes toward our core programs — the HIV orphanage (Snehalaya), girl child orphanage, support for migrant workers, differently abled individuals, and children's blind school.

Every painting you purchase is not just art on your wall — it's a child's education, a family's meal, and a community's hope.

Mariamma James

Featured Artist

A retired nurse with over 35 years of healthcare experience, Mariamma James is a self-taught artist who pursued her childhood passion for painting after retirement. Under the guidance of multiple art teachers, she has become accomplished in acrylic and oil painting — specializing in landscapes and the natural world rendered in a hypnotizing burst of colour.
