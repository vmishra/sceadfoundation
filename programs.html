Our Programs

Creating lasting change across environment, education, and community development.

Environmental Sustainability

Restoring ecosystems, expanding green cover, and fighting climate change through grassroots action.

Plantation Drives

Large-scale plantation drives across Bengaluru, Anekal, Jigani, Bannerghatta, and rural Karnataka. Over 10,000 trees planted since inception, contributing to green cover expansion and climate change mitigation.

Lake Rejuvenation

Lake rejuvenation initiatives restoring water bodies in urban and peri-urban areas, helping preserve vital ecosystems and community water resources.

Carbon Sequestration

Carbon sequestration verified by CERA (Carbon Emissions Reduction Assessment), Mumbai. Our efforts are measured and certified for real environmental impact.

Partnerships & Collaborations

Partnership with Genpact for Mahatma Gram Panchayat Plantation Drive. Peace tree planting with Consul General of Japan and St. Joseph's Institutions (2023). Collaboration with IPYG (International Peace Youth Group) for environmental initiatives.

Education & Child Welfare

Protecting children's rights and ensuring access to quality education for the most vulnerable.

Government School Adoption

Adopted Government Kannada & Tamil Schools in Cleveland Town, Robertson Road, Frazer Town, Bangalore since 15 February 2011. Activities include computer funding and training, library strengthening, slum adoption near schools, support for handicapped students, arts training (dance, music, singing), physical training (rugby, football), and play materials provision.

Tamil Nadu School Adoption (2024)

Adoption of 3 Government Schools in Tamil Nadu (Badrahalli) — a 2024 initiative expanding SCEAD's educational reach beyond Karnataka to ensure quality learning for underserved communities.

Rescue & Rehabilitation

Street children rescue and rehabilitation — helping street kids get back to school. Summer school programs for underprivileged children in JP Nagar and Koramangala slums, providing learning opportunities during school breaks.

Anti-Child Labour

Anti-child labour awareness campaigns — protecting children from exploitation in brick kilns and hazardous industries. Advocating for children's rights and safe environments.

Health Screening

Blood group identification and basic health screening programs for students, ensuring early detection and health awareness among young learners.

Shelter & Care

HIV orphanage (Home for HIV) providing shelter and care. Girl Child orphanage programs ensuring safety and education for the most vulnerable children.

Holistic Development

Art, creativity, and development activities for holistic child growth — nurturing talents beyond the classroom and fostering well-rounded individuals.

Snehalaya Children's Home Care Centre

Providing holistic care to HIV/AIDS-affected children for over 20 years.

SCEAD Foundation operates Snehalaya Children's Home Care Centre — a residential facility dedicated to helping each HIV/AIDS infected and affected child reach their fullest potential through holistic care. The home provides comprehensive support including physical care, medical treatment, psychological assistance, education, social support, spiritual guidance, and rehabilitation training — helping children lead healthier and better quality lives.

Our holistic approach addresses every dimension of a child's well-being — from nutrition and healthcare to education and emotional support — creating a nurturing environment where children can thrive despite their challenges.

Visitors Welcome: Groups, students, and volunteers are welcome to visit Snehalaya Children's Home to interact with the children, learn about our work, and contribute to their growth and happiness.

Girl Child Orphanage & Save Girl Child Campaign

Protecting vulnerable girls and creating safe environments for their education and growth.

SCEAD Foundation runs a dedicated Girl Child Orphanage program, providing shelter, education, and comprehensive care to orphaned and abandoned girls. Our "Save Girl Child Campaign" works tirelessly to protect vulnerable girls from neglect, abuse, and exploitation.

The program supports adoption of orphans into loving families and creates safe, nurturing environments where girls can access quality education, develop life skills, and grow into confident, empowered individuals.

Through community outreach and awareness drives, we advocate for the rights and dignity of the girl child — ensuring every girl has the opportunity to learn, grow, and lead.

Community Development & Women Empowerment

Building stronger communities through livelihood support, sanitation, and women-led initiatives.

Sanitation Infrastructure

Sanitation and toilet construction for tribal communities and slum dwellers — ensuring hygiene, dignity, and health in underserved areas.

Livestock Distribution

Distribution of cows, calves, sheep, and goats to tribal families in Karnataka for sustainable livelihood, enabling families to build long-term income and food security.

Skill Development

Skill development and tailoring training for women, empowering them with practical skills for economic independence and self-reliance.

Saukhyam Pad Training

Reusable sanitary pad production training (Saukhyam) for tribal women in Bandipur (2024-2025) — promoting menstrual hygiene awareness and income generation.

Peace & Social Harmony

Community welfare, peace initiatives, and social harmony programs bringing together diverse communities for collective progress.

Elderly Care

Ensuring dignity, health, and companionship for senior citizens across communities.

Comprehensive Care

Elderly care services across tribal, rural, and urban communities. Providing food, companionship, health checkups, and dignity to senior citizens who need it most.

CSR Partnership

Powered by Ascend Telecom CSR partnership (2023-2024), enabling us to extend our elderly care reach across Karnataka.

Wide Reach

Serving elderly in tribal hamlets, rural villages, and urban slum areas — ensuring no senior citizen is left without care and support.

Disaster Relief

Rapid response and relief during crises and natural disasters.

Turkey Earthquake Relief (2023)

Collected and shipped relief materials for earthquake-affected children in Turkey, sponsored by Genpact and coordinated with the Turkish consulate. A global act of solidarity and compassion.

Community Crisis Management

Community crisis management support during natural disasters — mobilizing resources, volunteers, and relief materials to affected communities when they need it most.

Key Events & Campaigns

Impactful events and campaigns that have driven awareness and change across communities.

Shoes and Smiles (2016)

Distributed shoes and socks to 250 underprivileged children at Govt Patel School, NS Palya, supported by Mantri Elegance residents community.

Hiroshima Day Peace Letter Campaign (2018)

Held at St Joseph's College of Commerce with international speakers from the Japanese Consulate and South Korean students. Partnership with HWPL and IPYG.

Cancer Awareness Program (2019)

Music therapy by Swedish musician and talk by Dr. Niti Raizada, Senior Consultant Medical Oncologist at HCG Cancer Centre, Bangalore.

Right to Food Campaign (2015)

Campaign at Town Hall, Bangalore demanding nutritious meals in government schools for underprivileged children.

COVID-19 Relief (2020)

Weekly distribution of soap and hand sanitizer to vulnerable communities. US Secretary of State Mike Pompeo tweeted about SCEAD's COVID relief — distributing 12,000 kg of food and essential supplies.

Peace Education at Candor International School (2019)

Kathak dancer Amita Mathur and classical musician Ghatam Giridhar Udupa performed. Supported by IPYG and HWPL.

Key Projects

Highlights from our flagship initiatives creating measurable impact across India.

Empowering Tribal Women Through Reusable Pad Production

Training tribal women in Bandipur to make and sell eco-friendly reusable sanitary pads for income generation and menstrual hygiene awareness. (2024-2025)

10 Years of Impact in Bandipur Tribal Village

A decade of work — providing goats and poultry for livelihoods, skill-building in sanitary pad and mosquito net production, toilet construction, water filter distribution, school adoption with new classrooms, free health camps, mobility aids (walkers, wheelchairs) for elderly, and agricultural development.

Adopted 3 Tribal Villages in Bandipur

Comprehensive village development — essential amenities, women's empowerment through self-help groups, elderly care with monthly food provisions, and school infrastructure. Also adopted 3 government schools in Badrahalli, Tamil Nadu (2024) for computer training, library, arts, and sports programs.

Turkey Earthquake Relief (2023)

Collected and shipped relief materials for earthquake-affected children in Turkey, sponsored by Genpact and coordinated with the Turkish consulate.

Elderly Care Service (2023-2024)

Providing elderly care across tribal, rural, and urban communities, powered by Ascend Telecom's CSR partnership.

Tree Planting with Japanese Consulate (2023)

A peace tree planting initiative with the Consul General of Japan and St. Joseph's Institutions in Bengaluru.
