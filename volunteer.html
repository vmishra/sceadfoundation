Why Volunteer With Us

Join 300+ volunteers from Poland, Germany, USA, Bahrain, Brazil, Australia, and 11 more countries

Work directly with tribal communities, government schools, and grassroots projects

Hands-on experience in sustainable development, education, and community work

Dedicated country coordinators help international volunteers plan their stay

Flexible: weekend drives, 1-6 month placements, or remote support

7 Ways to Contribute

Find the right way to use your skills for meaningful, community-driven work.

Education

Teach students, share music/sports/arts, train teachers, teach languages, create child labor awareness.

Environment

Plant trees across Bengaluru, Shillong, and Metur Dam. Rural farming, seed ball making, lake restoration.

Web & Marketing

Website improvement, social media, blog writing, online campaigns, and digital outreach.

Fundraising

Raise funds, identify sponsors, write grant proposals, and build corporate CSR partnerships.

Community Outreach

Tribal village visits, elderly care, sanitation campaigns, Shoes & Smiles drives, slum meal distribution.

Creative Arts

Paint school buildings, video documentation, art workshops for children, photography for impact reports.

Healthcare

Health screening camps, cancer awareness, HIV home visits, soap/sanitizer distribution in communities.
