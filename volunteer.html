Volunteer & Intern

Be part of the change — join SCEAD Foundation as a volunteer or intern.

Home / Volunteer

Why Volunteer With Us

  • Join a community of 100+ volunteers across India and internationally
  • Work directly with tribal communities, schools, and grassroots projects
  • Gain hands-on experience in sustainable development, education, and community work
  • International volunteers welcome — we've hosted volunteers from Japan, Europe, and more
  • Flexible volunteering: short-term drives, long-term placements, or remote support

Volunteering Opportunities

Find the right way to contribute your time and skills to meaningful, community-driven work.

Plantation Drives

Join our tree planting initiatives across Bengaluru, Anekal, and rural Karnataka. Help plant trees, maintain saplings, and support lake rejuvenation.

Education Support

Teach, mentor, or tutor underprivileged children at government schools. Distribute books and supplies.

Community Outreach

Participate in tribal village visits, elderly care programs, and sanitation awareness campaigns.

Event Support

Help organize fundraising events, CSR coordination, awareness campaigns, and disaster relief logistics.

Internship Program

Gain real-world experience while making a tangible difference in the lives of India's most underserved communities.

  • Open to college students and young professionals
  • Duration: 1-6 months (flexible)
  • Areas: Social work, environmental studies, public health, communications, fundraising
  • Certificate of completion provided
  • Opportunity to work on real projects with measurable community impact
  • Past interns have contributed to plantation drives, school programs, and CSR documentation
