Volunteer & Intern

Be part of the change — join SCEAD Foundation as a volunteer or intern.

Home / Volunteer

Why Volunteer With Us

  • Join a community of 300+ international volunteers from 15+ countries including Poland, Germany, England, USA, Egypt, China, Bahrain, Austria, Switzerland, Czech Republic, Netherlands, Sweden, Brazil, Russia, Singapore, Taiwan, Australia, Turkey, and Albania
  • Work directly with tribal communities, schools, and grassroots projects
  • Gain hands-on experience in sustainable development, education, and community work
  • International volunteers welcome — dedicated country coordinators help you plan your stay
  • Flexible volunteering: short-term drives, long-term placements, or remote support

Volunteering Opportunities

Find the right way to contribute your time and skills to meaningful, community-driven work.

Education

Teach local students, share talents in music, sports, crafts and arts. Train teachers, assist in classrooms, teach languages, and create awareness on child labor and social issues.

Agriculture & Environment

Plant trees, develop rural farming practices, and participate in plantation drives across Bengaluru, Shillong, and Metur Dam.

Web & Marketing

Improve the website, coordinate online promotion, manage blog and social media, and write articles about SCEAD activities.

Fundraising

Raise funds for the foundation, identify sponsors and corporate partners for CSR initiatives.

Community Outreach

Participate in tribal village visits, elderly care, sanitation campaigns, and Shoes & Smiles distribution drives.

Creative Arts

Paint and decorate school buildings, create video presentations of SCEAD projects, and organize art workshops for children.

Healthcare Support

Support health screening camps, cancer awareness programs, HIV home visits, and distribute soap and sanitizer in communities.

Internship Program

Gain real-world experience while making a tangible difference in the lives of India's most underserved communities.

  • Open to college students and young professionals
  • Duration: 1-6 months (flexible)
  • Areas: Social work, environmental studies, public health, communications, fundraising
  • Certificate of completion provided
  • Opportunity to work on real projects with measurable community impact
  • Past interns have contributed to plantation drives, school programs, and CSR documentation
