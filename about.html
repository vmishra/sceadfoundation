Who We Are

SCEAD Foundation India — Space for Children in Education, Art and Development — is a registered NGO founded in 1998 in Bengaluru by Siju Thomas Daniel.

Registration: GAN-04-00209 (Registered under Indian Govt)

Founded in 1998 by Siju Thomas Daniel, SCEAD Foundation India began as a humble initiative to support underprivileged children in Bengaluru. Over 25 years, we've grown into a multi-faceted development organization working across Karnataka and Tamil Nadu — touching lives through education, environmental restoration, women's empowerment, and elderly care.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To promote environmental sustainability, education, health, and community development among marginalized communities across India — empowering underprivileged children, tribal villages, women, migrant workers, and elderly citizens.

Our Vision

To build a sustainable, inclusive India where every child has access to education, every community has clean sanitation and green spaces, and every woman and elder lives with dignity and financial independence.

Our Journey

Over 25 years of grassroots impact across India.

1998

Founded

SCEAD Foundation established in Bengaluru to support underprivileged children's education and development.

2005

Expanding Reach

Began working with tribal communities in rural Karnataka, including Bandipur region.

2010

Environmental Focus

Launched large-scale plantation drives across Bengaluru, Anekal, Jigani, and Bannerghatta.

2014

Tribal Village Adoption

Adopted 3 tribal villages in Bandipur for comprehensive development.

2018

20 Years of Impact

Celebrated two decades with 5,000+ trees planted, 30+ villages served, and CSR partnerships established.

2020

Lake Rejuvenation

Initiated lake restoration and water body conservation projects.

2023

International Impact

Turkey earthquake relief, Japanese Consulate peace tree planting, and elderly care expansion powered by Ascend Telecom.

2024-25

Women Empowerment

Launched reusable sanitary pad production training for tribal women in Bandipur with Saukhyam initiative.

We believe that true development starts at the grassroots — when you empower a child with education, a woman with livelihood, and a community with dignity, you change the world one village at a time.
Siju Thomas Daniel
Managing Trustee & Founder

Siju Thomas Daniel has dedicated over 25 years to grassroots development across India. Under his leadership, SCEAD Foundation has planted over 10,000 trees, served 50+ villages, impacted 10,000+ lives, and built partnerships with leading corporates for sustainable CSR initiatives.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at SCEAD Foundation.

Grassroots Impact

We work directly with communities, ensuring every initiative reaches those who need it most.

Transparency

Complete financial transparency with certified audits, 10+ years of ITR filing, and open reporting.

Sustainability

Every project is designed for long-term impact — from tree planting to women's livelihood training.

Inclusivity

We serve all — children, women, tribal families, migrant workers, elderly citizens, and persons with disabilities.
